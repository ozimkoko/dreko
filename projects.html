29 Wells St — Garden Suite

Aurora, Ontario  ·  York Region
June 2026 Start · Nov 2026 Completion
Before — Current Property & Proposed Build Location

A purpose-built detached garden suite on an existing residential lot in Aurora. Designed from the ground up as a modern ADU with a focus on rental income generation, this project showcases Dreko's specialization in Ontario's evolving garden suite regulations.

The two-storey structure features a full apartment above a finished ground level, with charcoal metal siding, cedar accents, and a dark standing seam roof — a clean, contemporary aesthetic that complements the main residence while standing on its own.

Dreko is handling every phase: architectural design, permit application with the Town of Aurora, construction with in-house trades, and tenant placement upon completion.

586 Lake Dr S — Waterfront Seasonal Rental

Keswick (Georgina), Ontario  ·  Cook's Bay, Lake Simcoe
April 2026 Start · June 2026 Completion
Before — Current Property · Summer, Lakeside & Winter Views

A full-scope renovation and seasonal rental conversion of a lakefront property sitting just 20 feet from a 75-foot shoreline on Cook's Bay, Lake Simcoe. The vision: transform a traditional 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom cottage-style home into an attractive four-season destination rental — complete with a bunkie, a converted boat house, and a log cabin-style guest suite.

The renovation leans into a cozy, rustic lakehouse aesthetic: natural wood trims throughout, butcher block countertops, a new fireplace, restored original wood lap siding finished with traditional pine tar, and new windows oriented toward the water. The front and rear decks will be extended to create generous outdoor living space overlooking the lake.

The existing 400 sq ft stucco-faced garage is being converted into a log cabin-style bunkie, and the original boat house — which sits right on the shoreline and catches spray in windy conditions — is being restored into a unique waterfront room. A 35-foot wooden dock, Tesla/EV charging stations, updated landscaping, and full safety code upgrades round out the project.

Dreko Property Management handles all seasonal bookings, guest turnover, and ongoing maintenance — critical for a lakefront property that takes year-round environmental punishment from Lake Simcoe's winds, ice, and weather cycles.
