About This Suite

Brand new construction. Never lived in.

Step into a 700 sq ft detached suite that feels twice its size. Tall ceilings with recessed lighting, oversized European-style windows that flood every room with natural light, and a refined open-concept layout that flows from the kitchen through the living area and into both private bedrooms.

The kitchen is the centerpiece — soft sage cabinetry paired with warm butcher block countertops, a full stainless steel appliance package including range, dishwasher, fridge, and a stainless range hood. Modern subway tile backsplash and matte-black fixtures finish the space.

The bathroom is spa-style: a stunning double vanity with quartz counters and matte-black fixtures, a generous 3'×3' fully tiled glass shower, and a window that brings the outdoors in. The two-bedroom layout offers flexibility — a comfortable primary suite plus a second room ideal for a guest, home office, or nursery.

Every wall is sound-insulated for maximum comfort and quiet. Two designated parking spaces. A private rear yard area exclusively for you. In-suite laundry. And in a market where every dollar counts — all utilities are included.
