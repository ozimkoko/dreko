The Difference

Professional management
isn't just for high-rises.

High-rise standards. Neighbourhood scale.

Condo tenants get a management office, a maintenance team, and a standard of care. Tenants in a triplex get their landlord's cell number. That gap shouldn't exist — and with Dreko, it doesn't.

We bring professional property management to the space that's been overlooked: duplexes, triplexes, garden suites, and small multiplexes. The buildings where most of Ontario's rental growth is happening, but where professional management has never been the norm.

Let's be honest — tenants would rather deal with a professional management team than have their landlord show up to fix a leaky faucet. And landlords would rather not get eaten alive by $450 mini-split inspections and $300 toilet calls from outside contractors. Dreko solves both sides. We're the professional buffer that tenants want and the in-house trades team that landlords need — and because we are the contractor, every repair is priced fairly, done fast, and done right.

  • 01

    The Professional Buffer

    Tenants get a responsive, professional management team — not their landlord at the door. Landlords get peace of mind and controlled costs without being the point of contact for every issue.

  • 02

    Builder-Operator Model

    Most managers call a contractor. We are the contractor. In-house carpentry, plumbing, electrical, and HVAC means repairs get resolved — not escalated and marked up.

  • 03

    Tenant Interviews & Quality Placement

    We go beyond credit checks and references. Every prospective tenant sits down with us so we can assess fit — not just approval. Better tenants mean fewer issues and longer tenancies.

  • 04

    RTA Compliance & Tenant Privacy

    Proper N-forms, correct notice periods, LTB-ready documentation, and strict property access protocols. Tenant data is handled securely, and every maintenance visit is scheduled and documented.

  • 05

    Fair Maintenance Pricing

    Because we are the contractor, routine items like drywall repairs, paint, fixture swaps, and unit turnovers are priced at contractor rates — no middleman margins eating into your returns.

Beyond Maintenance

Proactive property care.

Most property managers wait for something to break. We look for what's about to — and what's quietly costing you money every month.

Every Dreko-managed property receives seasonal inspections — a structured walkthrough that covers the building envelope, mechanical systems, appliances, and common wear items. Think of it as a property health check that catches small issues before they become expensive emergencies.

Our energy and efficiency assessments identify problems that never make it into a standard maintenance report: drafty windows, underperforming HVAC systems, missing attic insulation, and appliances running harder than they should. We fix what we find with our own trades, and where upgrades qualify for Ontario rebate programs, we handle the paperwork too.

The result: lower utility costs, fewer emergency repairs, longer equipment lifespan, and a property that holds its value — not one that slowly deteriorates between tenant turnovers.
