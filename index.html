The Difference

Most managers call a contractor.
We are the contractor.

Property management companies outsource repairs. Contractors don't understand tenants. You end up coordinating between two worlds that don't talk to each other.

Dreko is both. We built and manage our own rental properties. We've navigated the LTB, run rent collection, screened tenants, pulled permits, and swung hammers — on the same buildings. That means faster maintenance response, smarter renovation decisions, and a manager who actually understands what's behind the walls.

  • 01

    Builder-Operator Model

    We don't just manage — we built these units. That means deep knowledge of every system, material, and design decision in your property.

  • 02

    In-House Trades

    Carpentry, plumbing, electrical, HVAC — no waiting for third-party quotes. Maintenance gets resolved, not escalated.

  • 03

    Ontario RTA Expertise

    We operate under the Residential Tenancies Act ourselves. Proper N-forms, correct notice periods, LTB-ready documentation.

  • 04

    ADU Specialization

    Garden suites and accessory dwelling units are our focus — from zoning review to occupancy. We know what municipalities want to see.
